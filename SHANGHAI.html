ABOUT THIS SITE

NYU Shanghai is a well known study away and J-term site. It is recommended for Chemistry, Economics, Engineering, History, Interactive Media, Legal Studies, Mathematics and Social Research and Public Policy.

NYU Shanghai offers a study away option for students interested in a semester or year studying in this exciting business and cultural center.
